Introduction to Kanban

Kanban is a tool for managing and optimizing workflow. Kanban can be useful for a wide range of people, from architects, to salepersons, to students. There are 3 rules for developing Kanban:
  • Visualize workflow
  • Limit work in process
  • Measure and improve workflow

Why Kanban?

  • Kanban creates flexibility for developer teams in planning since they only need to focus on active work and don't have to worry about outside changes.
  • Kanban minimizes the cycle time as all teams are responsible to keep the project going smoothly.
  • Kanban ensures efficiency by limiting work in process.
  • Kanban visualizes all metrics, allowing team members to easily catch up the workflow.
  • Kanban allows continuous delivery, and help the products delivered faster to the market.
